Mobile boarding pass

How to get your mobile boarding pass:

  1. Go to aa.com or your mobile app and choose ‘Email with Mobile Option’

  2. Follow the link in your email

  3. Save your boarding pass to your device for easier access through security

Flight notifications

BeNotified – alerts for all your flights:

Sign up or change your preferences from the ‘BeNotified’ link in your AAdvantage® profile to get alerts for every flight by text or email.

Choose alerts for:

  • Day-of-travel delays and cancellations

  • Departure reminders

  • Gate changes
